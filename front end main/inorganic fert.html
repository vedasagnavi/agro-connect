Fertilizers

Inorganic

1. Urea

Source: Synthetic nitrogen compound (CO(NH₂)₂)

Used For: Promotes fast leaf and stem growth in crops

How It Works: Supplies high nitrogen (~46%) — boosts chlorophyll and protein production

2. Ammonium Sulphate

Source: Manufactured from ammonia and sulfuric acid

Used For: Paddy, wheat, and sugarcane crops

How It Works: Provides nitrogen and sulfur — enhances green leafy growth

3. Ammonium Nitrate

Source: Industrial chemical (ammonia + nitric acid)

Used For: Cereal crops and vegetables

How It Works: Quick-release nitrogen fertilizer — supports rapid growth

4. Calcium Ammonium Nitrate (CAN)

Source: Made by mixing ammonium nitrate with limestone or dolomite

Used For: All soil types, especially acidic soils

How It Works: Supplies nitrogen and calcium — improves soil texture

5. Single Super Phosphate (SSP)

Source: Rock phosphate + sulfuric acid

Used For: Root crops and legumes

How It Works: Provides phosphorus, sulfur, and calcium — boosts root and flower growth

6. Triple Super Phosphate (TSP)

Source: Rock phosphate + phosphoric acid

Used For: Crops needing high phosphorus (like pulses)

How It Works: Provides about 45% phosphorus — improves root and seed development

7. Potassium Chloride (Muriate of Potash)

Source: Mined mineral (Sylvinite or Carnallite)

Used For: Fruit-bearing and root crops

How It Works: Provides potassium (K) — increases disease resistance and fruit quality

8. Potassium Sulphate (SOP)

Source: Manufactured from potassium chloride and sulfuric acid

Used For: Chlorine-sensitive crops (like tobacco, potato)

How It Works: Provides potassium and sulfur — improves yield and color

9. Diammonium Phosphate (DAP)

Source: Ammonia + phosphoric acid

Used For: All major crops

How It Works: Supplies nitrogen (18%) and phosphorus (46%) — strong root and shoot growth

10. Calcium Nitrate

Source: Reaction of limestone with nitric acid

Used For: Fruits and vegetables (especially tomatoes)

How It Works: Provides calcium and nitrogen — prevents fruit cracking and boosts firmness
